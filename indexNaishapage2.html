ALL ABOUT MAMMALS Did you know that all mammals except ant eaters have teeth or that the biggest mammal lives in the sea? If not not then you should surely read along! A panda is one of the most adorable and fascinating creature to date! The giant panda,also known as the panda bear, is a bear species endemic to China. It is characteriesed by its bold black-and-white coat and rotund body. The name "giant panda" is sometimes used to distinguish it from the red panda,a neighbouring musteloid.
